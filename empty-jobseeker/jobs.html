How to Use Job Searching Engine

By default you are seeing all the jobs posted on the platform, each with a unique Matching Percentage with your profile. It is always advisable that you have a complete profile so that the system can help you identify the perfect jobs for you. If you change the switch to 'Suggested Jobs', the system will provide you with all the jobs with a Matching Percentage of 50% and above.
