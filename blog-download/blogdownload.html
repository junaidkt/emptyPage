Why presentation skills can take you to the next step in the Dubai workplace

Posted By : BLOOVO admin 14 September, 2016

At one point or another we all have to prepare a presentation in front of others in the workplace, and offices in Dubai and the wider UAE are no different. Your manager may have asked you to display key findings to help with decision making, or the presentation may be a part of a project you are working on.

Similarly, presentations may be carried out to highlight new systems, software and technology or simply a change in direction or policy. They can be an excellent and highly efficient way of transferring data and ensuring that everyone is on board and following the same trail of thought, and as the staff member who has prepared it, you can reap the benefits via career progression.

However, being able to give an interesting and engaging presentation is a skill that requires practice and know-how, and is certainly not something to just jump into without prior knowledge of how to capture the attention of those in attendance. How do you hone such skills and what makes for a good presentation? Is it limited to the meeting room?

Why being able to present and articulate your ideas is so important

The role of presentations is becoming all the more important in the modern office and few forward-thinking and efficient workplaces operate without any form of them.
